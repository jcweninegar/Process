24-hour MVP guide

Process Documenter

Follow the 8-step, mobile-first flow to turn your idea into a documented MVP using ChatGPT Projects.

How to use this screen

  1. Open a ChatGPT Project for your idea.
  2. Expand a step, tap Copy Prompt, and paste it into ChatGPT.
  3. Keep ChatGPT writing to the markdown files named in each step.
  4. Stay on this page and work down the list until all 8 steps are done.
